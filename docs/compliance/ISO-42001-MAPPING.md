# ISO/IEC 42001:2023 Clause 8 artifact mapping

ISO/IEC 42001:2023 - Information technology - Artificial intelligence - Management system.

This document lists PEAC surfaces that can be used as supporting evidence toward ISO/IEC 42001:2023 Clause 8 (Operational planning and control) objectives. The scope is narrow: every row points at a concrete PEAC artifact (spec, fixture, test, package, or generated artifact) that an operator of an AI management system may reference when building their Clause 8 operational controls. Every row also states explicitly what PEAC does not do for that row; the operator is responsible for the operational decisions, risk treatments, and management-system activities themselves.

This document is not a certification. It is not a substitute for an AIMS implementation. It is not a claim that PEAC satisfies Clause 8 end-to-end. It is a mapping that helps an operator route a Clause 8 checklist to the underlying PEAC artifacts where supporting evidence exists.

## Scope and framing

- **Standard:** ISO/IEC 42001:2023.
- **Clause scope:** Clause 8 (Operational planning and control). Cross-references to Clauses 6.1 (Actions to address risks and opportunities), 7.5 (Documented information), 9 (Performance evaluation), and 10 (Improvement) are listed per row where relevant.
- **Artifact scope:** PEAC surfaces that an operator can reference as supporting artifacts. The operator owns the management-system activities.
- **Non-claim:** PEAC does not implement an AIMS. PEAC provides portable signed interaction records and verification surfaces that an AIMS can consume as evidence inputs. Risk assessment, risk treatment, impact assessment, decision-making, and management review are operator-owned.

## Column legend

- **Clause:** ISO/IEC 42001:2023 clause number and short objective phrase.
- **PEAC primitive:** the PEAC concept that produces the supporting evidence.
- **Artifact surface:** concrete file, package, spec, fixture, or API endpoint under this repository.
- **Coverage qualifier:** one of `supports`, `can be used as evidence toward`, `provides primary artifact for`, `provides supporting artifact for`, `helps evidence`, `out-of-scope for PEAC`.
- **Non-claim:** explicit statement of what PEAC does not do for this row; one of `PEAC does not do X`, `operator-owned`, `requires upstream attestation`, `evidence only; not a conformance claim`.
- **Cross-reference:** related ISO 42001 clauses and PEAC trust artifacts.

## What PEAC does not do for Clause 8

- PEAC does not implement an AI management system.
- PEAC does not perform AI risk assessment, risk treatment, or impact assessment.
- PEAC does not write organizational policy, make authorization decisions, or conduct management reviews.
- PEAC does not replace documented-information retention processes.
- PEAC does not schedule, plan, or conduct internal audits.
- PEAC does not determine conformity with any certifiable standard.
